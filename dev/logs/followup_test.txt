Q: salary of this?
A: The salary for the **System Support Officer (SSO)** is classified under **SPPP-5**. Additionally, the position may include any other benefits admissible to SPPP-5 as mentioned in Schedule-III.

For contractual employees, the salary and benefits are specified as SPPP-5, along with any other benefits

Q: he is reporting to?
A: The **System Support Officer (SSO)** reports to the **Sub Divisional Enforcement Officer (SDEO)** of the concerned Enforcement Station.
